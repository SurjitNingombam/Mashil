Who we are

Mashil-Shang is a counselling centre located at Nongmeibung, Imphal. We provide a wide range of counselling and therapies relating to mental health. 

We are registered under the LLP act,2008 and have clinical psychologists certified by RCI.

Clinical Services


We provide psychological assessments for children & adults. These range from learning issues to severe disorders. Clinicians also provide Psycho-education & Psychotherapy. We focus on promoting Mental Health, wellbeing, enhancing quality of life & de-stigmatizing Mental Health Conditions.

Counselling Services

Counselling is not just for people who are experiencing severe mental health distress, it serves a different purpose for everyone. At Karma, we provide a safe & open space for therapy & catharsis.

Child Specific Services

Cater to the various developmental, emotional & behavioural needs of children. Identification & effective management of common childhood issues: Developmental disorders, behavioural deviances, emotional disturbances, poor adjustment & academic performance at school, social anxiety & peer pressure/interaction.

Career Counselling

We can understand that selecting a career can cause a certain amount of worry & confusion. However, at Mashil-Shang, we aim to address & diffuse your doubts by providing you with the appropriate information to determine your career trajectory. Additionally, at Karma, we make this process a fun & insightful experience.

How to book a session

We are open from 6:00 am to 10 am and again from 3:00 pm to 6:00 pm during all the days of the week except Sunday, on which we are open from 9 am to 12am. Currently we are taking only offline booking, hence please come down to our office for booking your counselling session.
